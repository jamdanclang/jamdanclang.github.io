“Courage is what it takes to stand up and speak, it’s also what it takes to sit down and listen.” -Winston Churchill

I am a senior Advertising and Public Relations undergraduate at the University of Nebraska - Lincoln. In advertising I focus mostly on graphic design, but my degree has allowed me to declare three additional concentrations: English, Psychology, and Music. When I'm not busy working, I enjoy playing volleyball and basketball, jamming out on guitar and laughing about life in general.

I currently live and work in Neihardt Hall, UNL's Honors Residence Hall. As a Resident Assistant I get the opportunity to interact with a diverse group of students and help them develop a rewarding college experience. Working with University Housing and students has allowed me to plan and promote large-scale events as well as experience UNL and its opportunities to the fullest.

I am also very involved in the College of Journalism and Mass Communications at UNL. As a member of the Student Advisory Board I serve as a liaison between the student body and the faculty. The Student Advisory Board also develops events and policies to benefit different aspects of student life.

I spend a fair amount of my time working as a Student Designer for the CoJMC Marketing and Communications Director. My work here has ranged from helping to recruit students at New Student Enrollment to developing media for CoJMC's various events, forums and art displays. I have also made an interactive 4-year plan to help students plan out their classes while at CoJMC. I love this job because it allows me to create new and exciting things every day.
