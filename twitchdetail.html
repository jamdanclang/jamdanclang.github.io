An Advertiser's Guide to Twitch

For my masters thesis project, I created an interactive website that would serve as an advertising professional's introduction to the popular live-streaming website, Twitch. Check out the full project.

Twitch reaches a huge audience, yet it is an under-utilized advertising platform. This project solves four problems that I identified with advertising on Twitch:

  1. Advertisers and media buyers don't know about Twitch or why it's important
  2. The negative stereotypes of gamers and internet culture scare brands
  3. There are no widely accepted best practices for advertising on Twitch or identifying Twitch influencers
  4. There are few public examples of advertising success on the platform.
