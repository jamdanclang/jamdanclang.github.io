Bootleg Beer

Bootleg Brewers is a 20-barrel system brewery based in Taylor, Nebraska. This Sandhills beer oasis sits on a 40-acre beer garden, one of the largest in America. Bootleg came to me with a very specific style in mind for their beer labels and I'm glad I could deliver for them. With beer as good as theirs, I made sure they had my number for future projects!

This project included designing labels that met federal regulations for packaging and distribution. I worked with the brewers to create labels for beers as they were released. It's cool for me to be able to buy this western Nebraska beer to all the way in Lincoln, especially since I contributed to getting it here.
Find Bootleg Beer across Nebraska, or visit their website at bootlegbrewers.com.
