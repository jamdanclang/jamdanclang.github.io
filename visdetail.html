Visual Communications (JOMC 131, 132 and 133)

As a Graduate Teaching Assistant I had the opportunity to teach CoJMC's Visual Communications modules, which cover typography, layout design, photography, videography and web design.

As a member of the Vis Comm faculty, I taught 8 sections of ~20 students each over two semesters. Getting to interact with students was the best part of this graduate assistantship. In addition to in-class time, I also held lab hours to help students with their projects.
