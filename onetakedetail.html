One Take Wednesdays

This project was the result of a tendency to take music too seriously. I found when I was recording that I would settle for nothing less than perfection. To take some of the stress away from the process I decided to do One Take Wednesdays - a single rendition of a song published with mistakes for the world to hear.

You can listen to all of the recordings here!




Sundown Sundays - Save Art

A huge part of my life is music, so it only makes sense that I would want to make music of my own. I recorded some original songs in my basement, added a Taylor Swift cover for good measure and designed the CD case you see here. Sundown Sundays was created. Physical copies are available, but live performances are preferred.

Clang Guitars

When I said music was a huge part of my life, I meant it was a family affair. My grandfather, Nels Clang, made his first violin when he was 13. Our family has been building custom instruments ever since.

Pictured above are two custom guitars built in the "Guitar Garage". For the guitar in the second picture, I used automotive paint and airbrushes to bring the stars and stripes design to life.
Have a broken guitar? Shoot me an email!
