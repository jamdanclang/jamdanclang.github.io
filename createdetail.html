The Hustle

Aaron Draplin calls it the hustle: things you create but never get paid for. Let me tell you, the hustle is fun.

I'm what you might call a Pixel Pusher. I've made logos for my favorite community service projects, posters and stamps for my favorite local brew and enough band posters to fill a wall in my room. When a friend asks me if I can make them a logo, I'm happy to oblige.

In general there isn't much time for me to simply create, but I hope you enjoy what I've made time for. If you or someone you know needs something creative, hit me up. I'm always happy to work for something I believe in, or give someone an extra boost.
